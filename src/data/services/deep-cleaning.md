## What's Included in Deep Cleaning?

Our deep cleaning service goes beyond the surface. We tackle the areas that accumulate grime over time but often get overlooked in weekly routines.

### Kitchen Deep Clean

- **Appliances**: Inside the oven, microwave, refrigerator, and dishwasher
- **Cabinets**: Exterior wiped down, handles polished
- **Grout & Tile**: Deep scrubbing of backsplash and floor grout
- **Under Sink**: Organized and wiped clean

### Bathroom Deep Clean

- **Grout Scrubbing**: Between tiles in showers and floors
- **Fixtures**: Polished faucets, showerheads descaled
- **Exhaust Fans**: Vent covers removed and cleaned
- **Behind Toilet**: No corner left untouched

### Living Areas

- **Baseboards**: Entire home, hand-wiped
- **Light Fixtures**: Dusted and polished
- **Windows**: Interior glass, sills, and tracks
- **Under Furniture**: We move light furniture to clean beneath

### When to Book a Deep Clean

- **Spring cleaning** or seasonal refresh
- **Pre-party preparation** for hosting guests
- **New home move-in** before unpacking
- **Post-illness sanitization** for peace of mind
- **Haven't cleaned in a while** and need a reset

Our team arrives with professional-grade cleaners and equipment designed for deep scrubbing and sanitization. We allocate extra time to ensure every detail is addressed.

_Typical deep clean takes 4-6 hours for a 2-bedroom home._
