## Cleaning That Means Business

A clean office isn't just about appearances. It directly impacts employee health, productivity, and the impression you make on clients. Our commercial cleaning service keeps your workspace performing at its best.

### What We Cover

**Workstations & Desks**

- Surface wiping and sanitization
- Keyboard and phone area cleaning
- Monitor and screen dusting
- Desk organization tidying

**Common Areas**

- Reception and lobby cleaning
- Conference room reset and sanitization
- Hallways vacuumed and mopped
- Elevator and stairwell maintenance

**Kitchen & Break Room**

- Counters and tables wiped down
- Appliance exteriors cleaned
- Sink scrubbed and sanitized
- Floor mopped
- Supplies restocked (optional)

**Restrooms**

- Fixtures scrubbed and disinfected
- Mirrors polished
- Floor mopped with antimicrobial solution
- Supplies restocked (soap, paper towels, toilet paper)
